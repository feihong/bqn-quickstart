    +⟨1,2,3⟩ --> ⟨ 1 2 3 ⟩


### Add

    5+⟨1,2,3⟩ --> ⟨ 6 7 8 ⟩


### Negate

    -⟨1,2,3,4⟩ --> ⟨ ¯1 ¯2 ¯3 ¯4 ⟩


### Subtract

    "abcd"-32 --> "ABCD"
